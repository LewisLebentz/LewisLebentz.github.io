You may have seen this dress already today, it has been dominating Twitter and has even been mentioned on the news.

To some people, it looks black and blue and to others it looks white and gold. What colour does it look like to you? Personally, I see black and blue.

The debate started after singer Caitlin McNeil posted the picture on Tumblr, asking her followers what colour they thought it was.

WIRED has explained the science behind the 'illusion'. The answer, in short, is that the dress is blue and black!
